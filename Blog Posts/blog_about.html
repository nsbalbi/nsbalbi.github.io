About Me

Posted: 7/28/2022, Updated: 7/28/2022

Hi! My name's Nick. I'm currently a graduate student in the MIT Chemical Engineering Department, where my research interests lie in polymer science, nanotechnology, and self-assembly. I also make coding-based art in my free time, and just recently (as of 7/22) put together this site to post some tutorials.

As for how I got into programming and generative art, I can't exactly pinpoint it, but here's a list of some resources and other random things that have influenced me along the way:

  • The Processing homepage
  • Daniel Shiffman's "Coding Train" Youtube Channel
  • The art and tutorials of Etienne Jacob
  • The majority of the games made by Zachtronics
  • Everyone who's a part of the generative art community on Twitter
